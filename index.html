How Money Flows

📥 Your Entry

You buy SUNO tokens (minimum 0.01 SOL). After 10% Solana transaction fee, your tier determines retention rate.

💰 65% → Round Prize Pool

Goes to the current round's winners (distributed 80/20 between top 5 tracks and voters)

🏦 35% → Permanent Treasury

Saved forever and grows perpetually. Only used for the 1/500 bonus prize drawings.

🎁 Your SUNO Tokens

Sent to your wallet immediately based on your tier's retention rate. These are yours to keep!

Game Rules

🎵 Track Requirements

Accepted formats: MP3, M4A, OGG, WAV, FLAC, AAC. One track per user per round.

⏰ Timing

5 minutes to submit tracks. Voting duration is dynamic based on total track length (sum of all tracks + 1 minute).

🗳️ Voting

Only voters who voted for the WINNER share the 20% voter pool. Split proportionally by contribution amount.

🏆 Winning

Top 5 tracks with most votes win. Your tier multiplier applies to your prize. Ties split the prize.

🎰 Bonus Prize

1 in 500 chance every round. First place winner gets the bonus added to their prize. Bonus amount is 2-20% of permanent treasury.

🔄 Continuous Rounds

New rounds start automatically. Prize pool starts fresh each round, but permanent treasury keeps growing!

Voter Strategy

💡 How to Maximize Voter Earnings
